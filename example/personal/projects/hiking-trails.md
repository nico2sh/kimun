# Hiking Trails

Trails to explore, mostly around Southern California.

## Done

### Mount Wilson Trail
- Distance: 14.5 miles round trip
- Elevation: 4,600 ft gain
- Did it with Alex on 2026-04-12
- Incredible views from the observatory at the top
- Pack extra water — the last 2 miles have no shade

## Planned

### Bridge to Nowhere
- Distance: 10 miles round trip
- Elevation: 1,200 ft gain
- Bungee jumping available at the bridge
- Best in spring when the water level is manageable

### Mount Baldy via Devil's Backbone
- Distance: 11 miles round trip
- Elevation: 3,900 ft gain
- Exposed ridge — avoid in high winds
- Snow possible Oct-May, bring microspikes

### Cucamonga Peak
- Distance: 12 miles round trip
- Elevation: 3,600 ft gain
- Wilderness permit required (free, self-issue at trailhead)

## Gear Notes

- New trail runners are breaking in well
- Need to replace the water bladder — leaking at the bite valve
- Trekking poles made a huge difference on the Mount Wilson descent
