# Companion Planting

Notes from research on companion planting for the [[garden-plan]].

## Good Companions

- **Tomatoes + Basil**: Basil repels aphids and may improve tomato flavor
- **Carrots + Onions**: Onions repel carrot fly
- **Lettuce + Radishes**: Radishes mature fast and loosen soil for lettuce roots
- **Rosemary + Beans**: Rosemary deters bean beetles

## Bad Companions

- Tomatoes + Fennel (fennel inhibits tomato growth)
- Beans + Onions (onions stunt bean growth)

## Timing

- Basil sprouts: 5-10 days
- Tomato seedlings: transplant after last frost
- Lettuce: can start early, cool-weather crop
- Radishes: 25-30 days to harvest
