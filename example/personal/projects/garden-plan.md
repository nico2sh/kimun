# Garden Plan

## Layout

Three raised beds along the south fence (4x8 each):

### Bed 1 — Tomatoes & Herbs
- Roma tomatoes (center)
- Cherry tomatoes (east end)
- Basil (companion, between tomatoes)
- Rosemary (west end)

### Bed 2 — Greens
- Lettuce (mixed varieties)
- Spinach
- Kale
- Arugula

### Bed 3 — Root Vegetables
- Carrots
- Radishes
- Beets
- Onions

## Watering Schedule

- Morning watering: 7am
- Deep water twice a week
- Drip irrigation planned for summer
