# Coffee Brewing Notes

## Pour Over (V60)

My daily method. Consistent results once you nail the variables.

### Recipe (1 cup)
- 15g coffee, medium-fine grind
- 250g water at 93-96C
- Bloom: 30g water, wait 30 seconds
- Pour in slow circles: 30s to 150g, wait, 30s to 250g
- Total brew time: ~2:30-3:00

### Grind Size Matters
- Too fine → bitter, slow drawdown (>3:30)
- Too coarse → sour, fast drawdown (<2:00)
- Sweet spot: like table salt

## French Press

Weekend lazy morning method.

- 30g coffee, coarse grind
- 500g water at 93C
- Steep 4 minutes
- Press slowly
- Pour immediately (don't let it sit in the press)

## Beans I Like

- Counter Culture "Hologram" — balanced, chocolate, fruit
- Onyx "Monarch" — rich, dark chocolate, walnut
- Local roaster's Ethiopian natural — blueberry, wild

## Connection to [[sourdough-starter]]

Both coffee and sourdough rely on fermentation. The parallels:
- Temperature control matters
- Consistency in process yields consistent results
- Small changes compound over time
