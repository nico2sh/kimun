# Sourdough Starter

## Feeding Schedule

- Feed every 24 hours at room temperature
- Ratio: 1:1:1 (starter : flour : water by weight)
- Use unbleached all-purpose flour
- Discard all but 50g before feeding

## Signs of a Healthy Starter

- Doubles in size within 4-6 hours of feeding
- Smells tangy/yeasty, not acetone-like
- Bubbly and active surface
- Passes the float test (a spoonful floats in water)

## Baking Notes

- Use starter at peak (just before it starts to fall)
- For best flavor, cold ferment the dough 12-24 hours
- Hydration: start with 70%, work up to 80%

## Recipes to Try

- Basic country loaf
- Sourdough pizza dough
- Sourdough pancakes (great use for discard)
